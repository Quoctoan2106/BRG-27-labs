# Session 2a – Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) Analysis

---

## Overview

A TCO analysis comparing two printers over a 5-year period to determine the most cost-effective option for an organisation.

---

## Printers Compared

| | Printer A | Printer B |
|---|---|---|
| **Model** | HP DeskJet 2755e | Brother HL-L2350DW |
| **Type** | Inkjet (Entry-Level Home) | Laser (Office Workgroup) |
| **Purchase Price** | SGD 49.00 | SGD 229.00 |

---

## Assumptions

| Parameter | Value | Source |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Comparison period | 5 years | Lab requirement |
| Pages printed per week | 750 pages | Lab requirement |
| Power on per week | 40 hours | Lab requirement |
| Electricity rate | SGD 0.33/kWh | SP Group Singapore |
| Paper cost per ream (500 pages) | SGD 5.90 | Local retailer |
